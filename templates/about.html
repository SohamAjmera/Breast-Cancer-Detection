About Breast Cancer Detection

Understanding Breast Cancer

Breast cancer occurs when cells in the breast grow out of control. These cells usually form a tumor that can often be seen on an x-ray or felt as a lump. Early detection of breast cancer is crucial for successful treatment and improved survival rates.

Early Detection Saves Lives

Regular screening and early detection are vital in the fight against breast cancer. When breast cancer is detected early, and is in the localized stage, the 5-year relative survival rate is 99%.

Our Technology

This project utilizes advanced artificial intelligence and deep learning techniques to analyze breast imaging. Our system employs an Attention U-Net architecture, which helps identify potential abnormalities in breast tissue with high precision.

How It Works

Upload your breast imaging scan, and our AI model will analyze it for potential abnormalities. The system will provide a detailed analysis, highlighting areas of concern and offering preliminary insights about the tissue characteristics.

Important Note

This tool is designed to assist in the detection process but should not replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult with healthcare professionals for proper medical evaluation and diagnosis.
